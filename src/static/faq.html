What is SelfServe Timeshare? ‚ñº

SelfServe Timeshare is a revolutionary commission-free platform that allows timeshare owners to sell or rent their properties directly to buyers and renters. Unlike traditional brokers who charge hefty commissions, our platform lets you keep 100% of your proceeds while providing all the tools you need for a successful transaction.

How is this different from using a timeshare broker or agent? ‚ñº

Traditional timeshare brokers typically charge 15-40% commission on your sale, which can cost you thousands of dollars. With SelfServe Timeshare, you pay only a low monthly subscription fee and keep every dollar from your sale or rental. You maintain complete control over pricing, communication, and the entire transaction process.

Is it really commission-free? ‚ñº

Yes, absolutely! We charge no commissions, no transaction fees, and no hidden costs. You pay only your monthly subscription fee, and everything you earn from selling or renting your timeshare is yours to keep.

How quickly can I list my timeshare? ‚ñº

You can create and publish your listing within minutes of signing up. Our streamlined process guides you through adding property details, uploading photos, and setting your price. Most listings go live within 24 hours.

What if I've never sold a timeshare before? ‚ñº

Our platform is designed for first-time sellers! We provide step-by-step guidance, best practice tips, and comprehensive support throughout the process. You don't need any special experience - just follow our proven system.

How do I price my timeshare competitively? ‚ñº

Our platform provides market insights and comparable sales data to help you price your timeshare effectively. You can also adjust your pricing anytime based on market response and feedback.

What happens when someone wants to buy my timeshare? ‚ñº

Interested buyers contact you directly through our secure messaging system. You communicate directly with them, negotiate terms, and handle the transaction. We provide guidance and resources, but you maintain complete control over the process.

Do you handle the legal paperwork and closing? ‚ñº

While we provide resources and guidance on the closing process, you're responsible for handling the legal aspects of the transaction. We recommend working with a qualified real estate attorney or title company for the closing, which is standard practice for any real estate transaction.

Yes! Our Basic plan and above allow you to list for both sale AND rental simultaneously. This maximizes your opportunities and helps generate income while you're marketing for a sale.

Renters pay you directly. We don't handle payments or take any percentage of your rental income. You arrange payment terms directly with your renters, giving you complete flexibility and control.

You handle all rental terms, deposits, and agreements directly with renters. We provide templates and guidance, but you maintain control over your rental terms and policies.
