SMS Program & Privacy Information

SMS Marketing Program

By providing your phone number and checking the consent box above, you explicitly agree to receive marketing text messages from SelfServe Timeshare about our commission-free timeshare services, exclusive deals, property listings, and platform updates.

Message Frequency & Rates

Message frequency varies based on market activity and promotional campaigns. You may receive up to 4 marketing messages per month. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-Out Instructions

You can opt out of SMS communications at any time:

  • Reply STOP to any message to immediately unsubscribe
  • Reply HELP for customer support information
  • Contact support@selfservetimeshare.com to request removal

Privacy Protection

We do not share, sell, or rent your mobile phone number or personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your data remains private and is used solely for SelfServe Timeshare communications.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?

List your timeshare, set your price, and connect directly with interested buyers or renters through our secure platform.

Are there any commission fees?

No! You keep 100% of your sale or rental proceeds. We only charge a monthly subscription fee.

How do I handle transactions?

You handle all transactions directly with buyers/renters. We provide the platform for connection and communication.

Is my information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal and listing information.
